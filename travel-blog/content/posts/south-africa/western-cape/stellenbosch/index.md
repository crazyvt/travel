## Overview
Rolling vineyards and historic gables east of Cape Town.

## Highlights
- Wine tram-style tastings (Franschhoek nearby)
- Jonkershoek Nature Reserve
- Church Street cafes

## Practical
Book tastings; designate a driver or use tours.
